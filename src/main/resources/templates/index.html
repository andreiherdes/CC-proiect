Notify Me

Have you ever had a problem with your car like a burglary or being lifted by the police due to parking in the wrong place?

With this app you can simply notify others if such situations are about to occur using their car registration number.

You can also receive notifications which helps you learn from mistakes and contribute to the good state of traffic.

Notify, be notified, improve rating and become better!
